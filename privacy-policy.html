Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Dive ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account or use social features, we collect:

  • Display Name: A name you choose to display to other users (optional)
  • Friend Code: A unique code generated for friend discovery
  • Email Address: Only if you choose to create a full account (optional)
  • Authentication ID: Generated by Firebase Authentication for account management

1.2 Usage Data

We collect data about how you use the App:

  • Session Data: Focus session duration, completion status, XP earned, and timestamps
  • Progression Data: Your level, total XP, current streak, highest streak, and total focused minutes
  • Currency: Shadow Essence and Shadow Crystals balances
  • Cosmetics: Owned cosmetic items and equipped avatar customizations
  • Preferences: Theme settings (light/dark mode)

1.3 Social Features Data

If you use social features, we collect:

  • Friendships: Friend relationships and friend request status
  • Challenges: Challenge participation, progress, and results
  • Public Profile: Display name, level, stats, and equipped cosmetics visible to friends

1.4 Technical Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Crash Reports: Error logs and crash information to improve app stability
  • Analytics: App usage patterns, feature usage, and performance metrics
  • Push Notification Tokens: To send you notifications (if enabled)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • Track your focus sessions and calculate progression
  • Enable social features (friends, challenges) when you opt in
  • Sync your data across devices when you sign in
  • Send push notifications (if you enable them)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. This includes:

  • Session history (last 100 sessions)
  • XP, level, and progression data
  • Currency balances
  • Cosmetics inventory
  • App preferences

3.2 Cloud Storage

When you use social features or sign in, we sync certain data to Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) for:

  • Cross-device synchronization
  • Social features (friends, challenges)
  • Data backup and recovery

3.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Authentication
  • Firestore security rules to prevent unauthorized access
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

4.1 Google Firebase

Our App uses Google Firebase services, which are subject to Google's Privacy Policy:

  • Firebase Authentication: For user authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: For cloud data storage
  • Firebase Analytics: For app usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For push notifications
  • Firebase Remote Config: For app configuration

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to load custom fonts. This may involve Google collecting your IP address. Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

5.1 Public Profile Data

If you use social features, the following information is visible to your friends:

  • Display name
  • Friend code
  • Level and total XP
  • Current and highest streak
  • Total focused minutes
  • Equipped cosmetics (avatar frame, badge)

5.2 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share data:

  • With your explicit consent (e.g., when you add friends)
  • With service providers (Firebase) necessary to operate the App
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics or push notifications in app settings
  • Control Social Features: Disable social features or remove friends at any time
  • Local-Only Mode: Use the app without creating an account (limited features)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account:

  • Your account and profile data will be deleted
  • Local data on your device will remain until you uninstall the app
  • Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for statistical purposes

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. Firebase services are hosted on Google Cloud Platform servers worldwide. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Significant changes will be communicated through the App or via email if you have provided one.
