Account Deletion Request

Last Updated: January 2025

If you would like to delete your Dive account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Send an email to stoegersly@web.de with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include your account information (email address or user ID if you know it)
  3. We will process your request within 30 days

Email us to request account deletion:

Request Account Deletion

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will delete:

  • Your account and authentication information
  • Your profile data (display name, friend code)
  • Your progression data (XP, level, streaks)
  • Your session history
  • Your social connections (friends, challenges)
  • Your cosmetic inventory

Note: Local data stored on your device will remain until you uninstall the app. Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for statistical purposes.

Questions?

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us at stoegersly@web.de.
